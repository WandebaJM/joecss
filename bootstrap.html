Welcome to UMU E-learning Platform

Welcome to Uganda Martyrs University's online Learning Management system portal. This is the home page our Learning Management System. From this page, you will be able to navigate to get your lecturers and courses. Online instruction at Uganda Martyrs System(LMS) which is the University e-learning official medium. we endeavor to use this LMS to provide you with insights, advice, and tools to create and enhance a collaborative and meaningful online learning environment for quality educational experience.


Measures imposed for stoping the spread of Covid-19 pandemic have forced universities in Uganda to look for options of teaching and learning through an online medium. Having been approved to run courses online in the academic year 2019/2020, by National Council for Higher Education, Uganda Martyrs University has adopted online teaching and learning and research through this medium. As the university offers its learning using online platforms it is mandatory for students and staff to use moodle for asynchronous (remote, self-paced) learning and other tools, like zoom and google meet for synchronous(live) learning.


On this page you will find your Faculty, School, or Institute on which you are enrolled. It is from this unit that learning and assessments at different levels are offered. To ensure that you can successfully be engaged, training in the use of this medium needed to allow you to be at the top of this so-called "new normal". As students, try to access the course and course resources online, complete assignments, and communicate with the instructor.

The teaching staff also have an opportunity here to create and deliver learning content, monitor and assess student performance, and effectively communicate with students in the learning process
